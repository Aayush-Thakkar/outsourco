Driven by a Passion for Truth.

For over a decade, I've been on the ground, chasing stories that matter. From political backrooms to the frontlines of technological disruption, my mission has been to provide clear, factual, and impactful reporting.

I believe that great journalism is about more than just reporting the facts; it's about telling the human stories behind the headlines. It's about holding power accountable and giving a voice to those who might otherwise go unheard.
